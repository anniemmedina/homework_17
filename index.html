About me.

A California native; I was forged by the tempestuous waters of an East Coast art education at RISD. I have an absurd love of: tiny “Kauai” things, thought-provoking books & my Ewok-dog named Honeybean. What was being a printmaker for 7 years at Gemini G.E.L. like? Maybe I will tell you someday. All I can admit for now is that I have an undying passion for art, design and psychology and love teaching these topics to my adult students. I am currently giving my standard 110%, to growing as an artist and designer through UCI’s UX/UI bootcamp & loving it.
